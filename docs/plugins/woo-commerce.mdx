## Installation

To start using Tonder's plugin with WooCommerce on your website, you need to perform the following steps:

1. Have WooCommerce integrated into your site.
2. [Install the Tonder plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/tonder-payments-gateway/).
3. Navigate to **WooCommerce** > **Settings** > **Payments**.
4. Activate Tonder's plugin.
5. Click on **Tonder Pagos**.
6. Fill in your API Credentials and Tonder ID.

<Note>Your Credentials can be found in the **[Developers](https://stage.tonder.io/dashboard/developers/)** tab of Tonder's dashboard.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Integrate WooCommerce">
    To integrate Tonder Plugin, you need fist to integrate [WooCommerce](https://woo.com/) into your website.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the Tonder plugin">
    Access [Tonder's plugin page](https://wordpress.org/plugins/tonder-payments-gateway/) in Wordpress to download our plugin.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/plugins/woo-commerce/woo-tonder-install.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Tonder's plugin">
    To activate our plugin in Wordpress, do the following:
  
    1. Navigate to **WooCommerce** > **Settings** > **Payments**.
    2.  Toogle on **Tonder Pagos**.
    3. Save your changes.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/plugins/woo-commerce/woo-settings-gif.gif)
    </Frame>
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Tonder Pagos">
    You need to add your API credentials and Tonder ID. To perform this, follow the steps below:

    1. Click on **Tonder Pagos**.
    2. Fill in your API Key and Tonder ID as **Business ID**.
    3. Select the correct Environment.
    4. Save your changes.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/plugins/woo-commerce/woo-tonder-credentials-gif.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done!">
    You are fully integrated with our plugin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>**Please make sure to conduct all necessary tests afterward.**</Tip>
