Tonder is a payment orchestration platform that streamlines payment processing, supports various payment methods, and offers customizable integrations to enhance payment success, prevent fraud, and optimize customer experience.

<Frame>![](/images/home.png) </Frame>

## Benefits
Tonder offers a simplified and secure way of making online payments, enabling businesses to manage different payment methods, fraud, and processing costs more efficiently. Our solution involves a single integration that allows companies to accept all payment methods and manage fraud effectively, thereby improving payment and customer experiences. Tonder addresses common problems such as low approval rates, expensive development processes, and inadequate fraud management.
