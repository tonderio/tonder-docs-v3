In the Developers section of Tonder's dashboard, you will find your authentication credentials to integrate with Tonder's platform. These credentials are crucial to ensure that any interactions with Tonder's ecosystem are secure and authorized. 

Without proper authentication, you would not be able to access Tonder's services and features, which would hinder your ability to fully utilize the platform. Therefore, it is important to keep your authentication credentials safe and confidential to ensure the integrity of your account and data.
