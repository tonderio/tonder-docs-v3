## Revenue Optimization

Tonder's revenue optimization layer is a sophisticated system designed to streamline and optimize the integration and management of various payment services and processes in one single platform. This layer brings you the following features in our dashboard:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Connections" iconType="solid" icon="link" href="/dashboard/revenue-optimization/connections">
    Manage your connections to various payment providers and set a default payment route for your transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Routing" iconType="solid" icon="route" href="/dashboard/revenue-optimization/routing">
    Process the payment transactions by directing them to the appropriate payment processors or acquirers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Online Payments

Tonder Online Payments offers merchants a streamlined and secure solution for conducting online transactions. Along with facilitating smooth and protected payment processing, this brings you the dispute feature in the dashboard. giving you full control over your disputed transactions directly in the dashboard.
