This page provides a quick reference to the currencies available for transactions at Tonder. Each entry in the table includes the currency code, the full name of the currency, and the country primarily using it.

| Name               | Country Code([ISO 3166-1 Alpha 2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1_alpha-2)) | Currency         | Currency Code([ISO 4217 Alpha 3](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_4217)) |
|--------------------|--------------|------------------|---------------|
| United States      | US           | US Dollar        | USD           |
| Dominican Republic | DO           | Dominican Peso   | DOP           |
| Mexico             | MX           | Mexican Peso     | MXN           |
