Tonder's orchestration layer is a sophisticated system designed to streamline and optimize the integration and management of various payment services and processes in one single platform.

This layer acts as a central hub, coordinating the interactions between Tonder's core payment processing capabilities, external payment service providers, fraud detection systems, and any other involved technologies or services. The primary goal is to ensure that all components work together seamlessly, enabling efficient transaction processing, enhanced security measures, and a superior user experience.

## Key features

Tonder's Orchestration Layer main features includes:

- **Unified Integration Point**: Merchants can easily integrate multiple payment methods and services through a single, unified interface, reducing complexity and saving time.
- **Dynamic Routing**: Automatically directs transactions to the most suitable payment processor or method based on predefined criteria such as success rates, transaction costs, and customer location. This helps optimize transaction success rates and minimize processing fees.
- **Advanced Fraud Detection**: Integrating multiple fraud detection and prevention tools, our advanced solution analyzes transactions in real-time to identify and mitigate potential fraud, providing a multi-layered approach to enhance security and protect against financial losses.
- **Compliance and Security Management**: Ensures that all transactions comply with relevant regulatory standards and security protocols, such as PCI DSS and GDPR, thus safeguarding sensitive customer data and reducing compliance burdens for merchants.
- **Real-Time Analytics and Reporting**: Merchants can access real-time analytics and detailed reporting capabilities to monitor transaction success rates, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize payment strategies.

## Benefits

Using Tonder's Orchestration Layer brings you a diverse set of benefits, such as:

- **Operational Efficiency**: Tonder's orchestration layer automates and optimizes payment processes, reducing manual effort and operational costs for merchants to focus on their business. 
- **Enhanced Transaction Success Rates**: Intelligent routing and real-time fraud prevention improves transaction approval rates, enhancing revenue potential.
- **Scalability**: With the orchestration layer, businesses can easily add new payment methods and services without significantly changing the existing infrastructure, making it a scalable solution for growing needs.
- **Improved Customer Experience**: Customers can now enjoy a seamless and secure payment experience on various platforms and devices, resulting in increased satisfaction and loyalty.
- **Risk Mitigation**: Advanced security features and compliance management capabilities minimize the risk of data breaches and fraud, protecting both merchants and their customers.
