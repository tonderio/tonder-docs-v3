### The-One-Stop Shop for your Payments

Tonder provides a powerful platform to make online payments faster, simpler, and
more secure. Many businesses that accept digital payments worldwide struggle to
keep up with payment integrations that offer all payment methods available in
the market while also addressing fraud and payment processing costs.

Tonder solves these problems by enabling any company to accept all available
payment methods and manage fraud and chargebacks a through a single integration.
Tonder offers brilliant payment and customer experiences by addressing issues
such as low approval rates, development cost processes, and poor
fraud/chargeback management.

### The Tonder Effect

- One single integration to connect all payment ecosystems and anti-fraud
  solutions.
- Route through different payment providers and increase payment success.
- Hundreds of payment services in one integration.
- A commercial and payments team to help you scale your business.

Building and managing your payment stack has never been easier with Tonder. You
can connect dozens or even hundreds of payment methods with just one
integration. And the best part? If there's any payment provider you want to
offer your clients, just let us know, and we'll release new integrations every
single month. Tonder is innovating to simplify your payment processes and give
your customers the best checkout experience possible. Innovative payments;
limitless possibilities.
