The Tonder Plugin for WooCommerce is a powerful integration tool that seamlessly connects your WooCommerce-based online store with Tonder's payment orchestration platform. 


https://wordpress.org/plugins/tonder-payments-gateway/

<Frame>![](/images/woocommerceTonder.png)</Frame>


**Instructions**
1. You must have WooCommerce on your site.
2. Install and activate the Tonder plugin.
3. Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Payments. Active the plugin and then click on “Tonder Pagos”.
4. In this step, you must paste your API Credentials and Tonder ID from your dashboard.
